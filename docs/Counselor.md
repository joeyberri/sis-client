# Counselor (optional role)

Purpose: monitor student wellbeing, interventions, counselling notes.

## Sidebar

-   Dashboard
    
-   At-risk Students
    
-   Student Profiles (counseling view)
    
-   Appointments
    
-   Interventions
    
-   Reports
    
-   Messaging
    
-   Resources
    

* * *

### At-risk Students

-   List sorted by risk score (attendance + grades + behavior)
    
-   Filters: grade-level, risk type
    
-   Actions: create case, schedule meeting, notify guardian, refer
    

### Student Profile (counselor view)

-   Counseling notes (private)
    
-   Intervention history & outcomes
    
-   Appointments & follow-ups
    
-   Consent & privacy flags
    

### Appointments

-   Book/cancel sessions
    
-   Calendar view
    
-   Notes per session
