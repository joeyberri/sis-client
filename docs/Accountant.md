# Accountant / Finance (optional role)

Purpose: manage school finances, fees, invoices, payment reconciliation.

## Sidebar

-   Dashboard
    
-   Fees & Schedules
    
-   Invoices
    
-   Payments & Reconciliation
    
-   Reports
    
-   Discounts & Scholarships
    
-   Settings
    

* * *

### Fees & Schedules

-   Create fee items (term fee, exam fee)
    
-   Assign to students or groups
    
-   Bulk invoicing
    

### Invoices

-   Outstanding invoices table: Student | Invoice # | Amount | Due Date | Status
    
-   Bulk remind, apply payments
    

### Payments & Reconciliation

-   Import bank statements
    
-   Reconcile payments, tag disputes
    
-   Export financial reports (CSV/PDF)
